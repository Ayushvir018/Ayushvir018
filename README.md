## üöÄ Featured Project: UMEED
**UMEED (Under-surface Mapping for Emergency Evacuation & Detection)** is an IoT-based mine safety system designed to protect workers during sudden rockfall incidents. It continuously monitors underground conditions and detects rockfalls instantly using high-frequency vibration sensors. Once a hazard is detected, an autonomous drone is deployed to assess the site and assist in rapid localization. Smart helmets provide haptic alerts, SOS communication, and GPS-denied positioning, guiding workers toward safe evacuation routes. Together, UMEED delivers faster detection, quicker response, and safer rescue, reducing emergency time from hours to minutes.
